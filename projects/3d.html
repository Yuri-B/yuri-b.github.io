3D Models

2000 - Today

From 2000 till 2008, I occasionally learned 3D modelling. Around 1997, I saw a TV programme about Visual Effects. I thought it was magic. So back in the year 2000, I managed to get my hands on a few 3D programmes and started learning them. I have stuck with 3D Studio MAX, and here is some of the work that I have produced.

Speech Bubble Shelf ( 2008 )

A rendering of a plastic shelf that looks like a speech bubble. Made with 3D Studio Max and composited in Adobe Photoshop.

Reneissance Scene (2007 )

The aim was to create a Reneissance-style scene, where a person crawls out of a painting and sits on a chair. The project was not fully completed.

Flowerpot ( 2007 )

A flowerpot shaped like a lab technician with a test tube. I have used a blueprint to develop a 3D model.
