About Me

First and foremost, I bring creativity, systems thinking and absurdist humour to all my work. I'm comfortable with doing both deep theoretical and practical subject matter. I'm currently learning the art of communicating technology and new ideas in an accessible and visual way. I have found that my main skill of design conceptualization and idea visualization helped non-technical professionals to uncover new opportunities.

I have previously worked in start-ups, where I specialized in Prototyping of software and hardware applications. I have utilized hardware tools such as CAD, 3D Printing, mechanical design and laser cutting, that I have learned in Product Design course. I've also worked in software companies, where I have used self-taught web development skills ( JavaScript, jQuery, HTML5, CSS3, SEO, HubSpot ). This experience helped me to gain insight into software development and electronic data.

In spite of experiencing break up of Soviet Union, conflict between two rival streets in Dundalk, working in start-ups for over seven years and moving to a different continent, I'm still in one piece.

Timeline

  • Born in Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic

  • 1996/1997 Saw a TV programme where a person with a stylus was tracing a sculpture and a 3D image appeared on the screen. I thought it was magical.

  • 1997 Moved to Ireland

  • 2001 - 2008 Videogames, album covers and cartoon.
    I have made an attempt to create videogame with my classmates. During that time, I have produced various artwork, concept art, 3D graphics, and animations. I also been commissioned to create concert posters by local bands "Take the Money and Run" and "Second Nature". I've also made a brief animated cartoon

  • 2007 - 2011 B.Sc. Product Design ( Dublin Institute of Technology )
    After unsuccessful attempt to create video games and animations, I have followed advice to channel my skills towards something "serious" that could potentially make me earn a living. During my studies, I had major exposure to engineering, CAD software and design.

  • 2010 Nominated for Red Dot design award
    Collaborated on an experimental project to decompose cigarette butts.

  • 2011 Got mentioned in an Irish newspaper for a project
    Thesis project to reduce eye strain from computer monitors from college got mentioned in an Irish newspaper.

  • 2011 - 2016 Worked in start-ups in Dublin.
    Done a wide variety of projects, from 3D Printing to Interface Design and freelance work. Had an idea for a website creator app, and then joined a start-up which created a one of the first HTML5 mock-up tools. Up until 2014, I thought skeumorphics were amazing.

  • 2015 Took part in informing voters of Ireland about election candidates.
    Collaborated on development of a voting tool which was used in several public election campaigns in Ireland. I thought that by participating in improvements of politics and working in tech start-ups, I could get bragging rights to a cheap mortgage in Ireland

  • 2016 - 2018 Worked on retail and payment technologies in Vancouver
    Took part in building software solutions for retail and payment technologies markets. Also done some freelancing, mainly for one exciting property technology start-up.

  • 2018 - 2019 Explored future project directions
    I was running MakerEngine on the side. I have briefly worked on educating artists on more cost efficient ways to certify their art for re-sale with blockchain technology.

  • 2019
    From April to October, I have worked with Fathym to develop a more user friendly way of building Internet of Things. I have also taken part in development of engineering projects with i-onCONNECT, where I have consulted on solutions to product design and development issues.

  • Present
    Working on better ways to manage electronic data. Learning 3D modelling and technical illustration at VCAD.
